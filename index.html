Rosemary “Ro” Dutton, a newly tenured literature professor, falls into a whirlwind romance with Ash, a charismatic artisan whose perfect cottagecore life hides dangerous secrets. As their relationship deepens, Ro’s world shrinks to fit Ash’s idyllic orbit, and the line between love and obsession blurs against a backdrop of creeping, maybe‑supernatural dread. The novella leans into psychological horror and folk‑ish unease more than jump scares, turning cozy vibes into something quietly menacing.

Content warnings: Psychological horror, gaslighting, obsession, manipulation, implied supernatural violence, toxic relationship dynamics, body‑centric horror.
